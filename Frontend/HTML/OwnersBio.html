Rayan Rashid

Rayan Rashid has revolutionized the food scene at University of Houston Sugar Land. As a current CIS student at the University of Houston, Rayan noticed the campus lacked in tasty, affordable eats. Determined to make a change, he took matters into his own hands, teaming up with the University to bring delicious, budget-friendly fare to hungry students.

Rayan's journey into the culinary world started with a simple desire to fill a need. With a knack for flavors and a passion for innovation, he crafted a menu that speaks to the student palate while keeping wallets happy. From mouthwatering bites to comforting classics, every dish is a testament to his dedication to quality and affordability.

When he's not whipping up culinary delights or hitting the books, Rayan can be found enjoying downtime with friends, exploring new food trends, and dreaming up the next big thing for his beloved campus eatery. Join him on this flavorful journey, where every bite tells a story of passion, dedication, and deliciousness.
